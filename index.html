General Dentistry

Prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of common oral health issues, including cavities, gum disease, and routine cleanings through regular check-ups, restorative procedures, and patient education.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are a permanent solution for missing teeth, fusing with the jawbone to provide a strong, natural-looking, and long-lasting replacement.

Complete Dentures

Complete dentures are removable prosthetic teeth that replace all missing teeth in the upper or lower jaw, restoring function, speech, and appearance for improved oral health.

Dental Extraction

Dental extraction is the removal of a tooth due to decay, infection, or crowding, helping relieve pain and prevent further oral health complications.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment removes infected pulp from a tooth, cleans and seals the canal, preserving the tooth structure while relieving pain and preventing further infection or damage.

Orthodontic Treatment

Orthodontic treatment uses braces or aligners to correct misaligned teeth and jaws, improving bite, function, and appearance for better oral health and aesthetics.
